Understanding Lightspeed X and WooCommerce

Lightspeed X is a modern cloud-based retail POS system designed to help businesses manage inventory, sales, and customer relationships efficiently. It is widely used by retailers for its flexibility and scalability.

WooCommerce, on the other hand, is one of the most popular eCommerce platforms built on WordPress. It allows businesses to create customizable online stores with a wide range of plugins and themes.

While both platforms are powerful individually, integrating them unlocks a new level of efficiency by synchronizing data between your physical and online stores.
